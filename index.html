Project Overview

Welcome to the client site of Team Xtreme.As Team Xtreme we redesigned the Comp 1950 Intermediate Web Development and Design, course website. The COMP 1950 owner Alistair Calder would like the web site redesigned in order to better meet the needs of students, by working at points, like an online textbook, also the instructor would like to improve the accessibility of information on the site while still keeping to the general setup of weekly lectures and notes. Overall, we tried our best to please not only students but the instructor as well, by achieving the goals required.
